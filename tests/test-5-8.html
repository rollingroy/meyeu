Test Chapter 5 - 8

Sunday, November 22nd 2020

1. The promise that God and his people made is called the _________.

  1. Special Promise
  2. Covenant
  3. Commandment
  4. Sign of the Cross

2. We call the sin of our first parents ___________.

  1. Original Sin
  2. bad sin
  3. the worst sin
  4. first sin

3. Jesus told his friends, "____________."

  1. Be good
  2. Try hard
  3. Let's have fun
  4. Love one another

4. Jesus would save people ________.

  1. from their sins
  2. from evil
  3. from dying
  4. from pain

5. Jesus' death on the cross is called the _________.

  1. Ascension
  2. Crucifixion
  3. Resurrection
  4. Assumption

6. The ___________ is when Jesus rose from the dead to new life.

  1. Resurrection
  2. Pentecost
  3. Ascension
  4. Assumption

7. ____________ was one of the first disciples of Jesus.

  1. Jonah
  2. The pope
  3. Mother Teresa
  4. Mary Magdalene

8. ____________ are prayers of praise.

  1. Petitions
  2. Intercessions
  3. Hymns
  4. Acclamations

9. God sent __________.

  1. a special messenger
  2. his Holy Spirit
  3. his Holy Trinity

10. _______________ is the Third Person of the Holy Trinity.

  1. St. Paul
  2. Jesus
  3. God the Father
  4. The Holy Spirit

11. We call the return of the Risen Jesus to his Father in heaven the ____________.

  1. Resurrection
  2. Assumption
  3. Incarnation
  4. Ascension

12. Mary is _________________.

  1. special
  2. the Mother of the Church
  3. sad
  4. a popular name

13. ___________ is the day the Holy Spirit came to the disciples of Jesus.

  1. Easter
  2. The Incarnation
  3. Christmas
  4. Pentecost

14. At Pentecost __________ settled over the disciples' heads.

  1. little birds
  2. halos
  3. small flames
  4. doves

15. We ask the Holy Spirit to help us to _______________.

  1. be happy
  2. do our homework
  3. obey our parents
  4. live as children of God

16. The Catholic Church is ________________.

  1. small
  2. a community
  3. a big building
  4. our parish

17. The people in the Catholic Church share the same faith and __________.

  1. trust
  2. Parishes
  3. work
  4. sacraments

18. St. Paul tells us that the Church is _______________.

  1. big
  2. small flames of fire
  3. the Body of Christ
  4. our parish building

19. ____________ is the head of the Church.

  1. The pope
  2. The Holy Spirit
  3. God the Father
  4. Jesus Christ

20. The _________________ is all the faithful people of God.

  1. Communion of Saints
  2. angels and saints
  3. town of Bethlehem
  4. diocese
